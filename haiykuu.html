Haikyuu

What is Haikyuu?

Haikyuu is a Japanese sports manga and anime about volleyball that began in February 2012 to July 2020, written and illustrated by Haruichi Furudate. The story follows protagonist Hinata Shouyo on his journey to become like his idol, The Little Giant, and prove that he can compete in volleyball without height.


Why Do I Like Haikyuu?

The Realism

Haikyuu holds a special place in my heart because of the realistic moments within the series, and the moments most grounded in reality. There are many highs and lows, as there is with all sports, and the way the characters bond and act and react with each other during the highs and lows feels genuine. I play volleyball in real life and watching the show is like a breath of fresh air, because it can be exagerrated sometimes but it also has some extremely real moments. Haikyuu compels you to ask yourself "who will you be tomorrow?" and encourages you to jump higher and look for new ways to fly in your life.
