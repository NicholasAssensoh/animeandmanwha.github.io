Chainsaw Man

What is Chainsaw Man?

Chainsaw Man is an Japanese action-adventure comic series created by Tatsuki Fujimoto in December 2018, and is still running currently on its second part. Chainsaw Man follows main protagonist Denji, as he originally lives his life in poverty trying to pay back his father's debt. His life changes after he is exposed to the Chainsaw Devil and he becomes Chainsaw Man, who lives his life killing devils. It received an anime adaptation in 2022 and it was animated by Studio Mappa, to which it was widely enjoyed by fans around the world.


What Is Unique About Chainsaw Man?

The Themes

Chainsaw Man is truly one of the most unique works to ever be published by Shounen Jump and it's hard to say what exactly makes it so different. The themes portrayed in Chainsaw Man are some of the things that have spoken to fans the most: themes of depression, trauma, control, family, freedom, death, loss and many more. Chainsaw Man is very fast-paced but somehow it portrays each of these themes extremely well through the amazing and diverse characters, and the way they interact.
