Tokyo Ghoul

What Is Tokyo Ghoul?

Tokyo Ghoul is a psychological Japanese comic series by Sui Ishida that began in 2011. It follows main protagonist Ken Kaneki who lived a normal life until a tragic accident turn him into a human and ghoul hybrid, a creature that consumes the flesh of others. Throughout the series, Kaneki struggles with balancing his new life as half human, half ghoul.


Why Is Tokyo Ghoul My Favourite Manga?

The Duality

One of the best things about Tokyo Ghoul are the themes of duality. Kaneki's significant character changes are greatly represented by his change in hair colour, due to his Marie Antoinette syndrome. Kaneki balancing his humanity and his ghoul side is the untimate testament to his character and watching him accept both sides as well as redefine what it means to be human and what it means to be ghoul is what makes Tokyo Ghoul a truly memorable piece.
