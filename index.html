The God of Highschool

What Is "The God of Highschool?"

Created by Yongje Park in 2013, The God of Highschool is a Korean comic book and web series that follows high school protagonist Jin Mori on his journey to win the G.O.H tournament and be recognised as the best fighter.


What Makes The God of Highschool Special

Martial Arts

If there's anything iconic about GoH, it's the various martial arts and fighting styles featured in the collective 570 chapters. This includes, but is not limited to, kokushin karate, taekwondo, taekkyon, muay thai, and many more. As it is an action comic the fights are the juiciest parts of the series.
